Fronteers

Since 2012 am I a member of Fronteers, a Dutch non-profit trade organisation for front end developers. They organize monthly meetings, workshops and the annual Fronteers Conference. If you are a Dutch or Belgian front end developer, check out the Fronteers website for meetings and workshops.

In this section you will find my notes of the meetings and the Fronteers conferences I attended.
